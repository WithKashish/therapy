EXPRESSIVE ART WORKSHOPS

Our Expressive Arts Workshops are all about unleashing emotions not just through words, but through diverse art forms. Imagine moving, dancing, creating music, or drawing – sometimes even blindfolded – with a group of people you barely know, and yet, connecting on a deeply emotional level.

I’ve facilitated these transformative workshops pan-India, reaching incredible individuals from various backgrounds and life experiences. We’ve explored healing with moms, LGBTQIA+ community, created safe spaces for domestic violence and abuse survivors, and brought powerful experiences to educational institutions like IMT Ghaziabad and IIT Delhi.

01

Unleashing Anger & Healing (For the Transgender Community)

This expressive arts workshop opened a safe, trusting space for transgender individuals. Through guided imagery, tearing paper, art, and movement, participants were able to release some long-held anger and frustration, finding new ways to talk about their experiences and emotions within a deeply supportive community.
