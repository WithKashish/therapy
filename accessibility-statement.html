Accessibility Statement

This statement was last updated on 8th July 2025.

WithKashish is committed to making mental health care accessible to everyone.

At WithKashish, we are committed to making this website and therapy experience as accessible, inclusive, and supportive as possible.

We understand that access looks different for everyone — whether it's related to technology, communication, mobility, sensory experience, or emotional capacity. Our goal is to create a space where anyone seeking support can navigate with ease, comfort, and dignity.

Website Accessibility

We aim to make the website user-friendly for all visitors by:

  • Ensuring compatibility with screen readers and assistive technologies
  • Using clear, legible fonts and appropriate contrast
  • Structuring content in a simple and intuitive layout
  • Adding alt-text to visual elements where applicable
  • Minimizing pop-ups and distractions

This site is best viewed on both desktop and mobile, and we are continually working to improve the experience across devices and platforms.

Therapy Access & Support

If you're someone who:

  • Uses assistive tools for vision, hearing, mobility, or cognition
  • Prefers communication in writing or at a slower pace
  • Needs flexibility with forms, session structures, or reminders
  • Finds typical digital experiences overwhelming or confusing

Please know that you're welcome to share what you need. You do not have to disclose a diagnosis or go into detail — your comfort and autonomy will always be respected.

We are open to making reasonable adjustments wherever possible, whether that's in communication, scheduling, or session delivery.
