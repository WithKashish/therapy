Privacy Policy

A legal disclaimer. At WithKashish, your privacy and emotional safety matter deeply.

1. What We Collect

  • Your name, email, contact number
  • Intake form data (only if provided by you)
  • Payment information (handled securely via third-party platforms)
  • Website analytics (via cookies, for improving user experience)

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used solely to:

  • Book, confirm, or remind you of appointments
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Facilitate payments
  • Comply with legal and ethical responsibilities
  • Improve website content and layout

We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

3. Therapy Session Confidentiality

Everything shared in session is held in strict confidence, with exceptions only when:

  • There’s a serious risk to your life or someone else’s
  • Abuse or neglect (especially of minors, elderly, or vulnerable adults) is disclosed
  • Required by court or legal process

4. Data Protection

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to safeguard your information. Exceptions apply only under the same circumstances listed above for confidentiality.

5. Your Rights

  • You can access, edit, or request deletion of your data
  • You can opt out of emails or analytics tracking
  • You may withdraw consent for therapy at any time (unless legally restricted)
